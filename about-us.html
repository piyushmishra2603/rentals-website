Our Story

From a Frustrating
Search to a Revolution

It started in June 2023 when our founder Piyush Mishra relocated to Hyderabad for work. Three months of apartment hunting revealed everything wrong with India's rental market: brokers demanding 1–2 months' commission, listings with fake photos, landlords who ghosted after taking token amounts, and no way to verify anything.
The problem wasn't just annoying — it was costing Indians ₹12,000 crore annually in broker commissions alone, on top of inflated rents and deposit disputes that had no resolution mechanism. Meanwhile, honest property owners struggled to find reliable tenants without expensive agents.
SmartRent AI was built to disrupt this. We combined AI-powered matching, mandatory KYC verification, transparent pricing, and direct owner-tenant connection into a single platform. No brokers, no hidden fees, no fake listings. Just intelligence applied to one of India's most broken markets.
Today, we've helped 50,000+ tenants find homes without paying a rupee in brokerage, saved ₹12+ crore in broker commissions, and enabled 10,000+ verified property owners to rent faster and smarter.
"Renting should be as simple as ordering food. We're making it happen."
— SmartRent AI Mission Statement
What Drives Us

Our Mission & Core Values

Every decision we make is guided by six principles that define who we are and how we serve the 300 million Indians who rent every year.

01 / MISSION
🎯
Fair Renting for All
To make renting in India completely fair, transparent, and intelligent — eliminating brokers, fraud, and information asymmetry from one of India's most important markets.
02 / TRANSPARENCY
🔍
Radical Transparency
Zero hidden fees. Zero fake listings. Every price, rule, and condition disclosed upfront. We believe that information freely shared builds trust that cannot be bought.
03 / TRUST
🛡️
KYC-First Culture
Every owner and tenant on our platform is identity-verified before any interaction. Trust isn't a feature — it's the foundation we build everything on.
04 / INTELLIGENCE
🤖
AI That Actually Helps
Our machine learning doesn't just match keywords — it understands commute, lifestyle, growth trajectory, and budget patterns to connect people with homes they'll genuinely love.
05 / SPEED
⚡
Speed as Respect
Rental searches waste months of people's lives. We've cut the average time-to-home from 90 days to under 14 days. Respecting your time is a core product principle, not a metric.
06 / IMPACT
🌍
India-Scale Thinking
300 million Indians rent. We build for everyone — from the student in a ₹5,000 PG in Ameerpet to the startup needing 20,000 sqft in BKC. Scale is our responsibility.
