Hiring Process

No Surprises,
No Nonsense

We respect your time. Our process is fast, transparent, and designed to let you do your best work.

01
📋
Apply
Submit your application on this page. We read every single one. No automated rejection — real humans review your background within 48 hours.
02
☎️
Intro Call
A 30-minute call with someone from the team (not just HR). We'll tell you what we're building, what the role actually involves, and answer any questions honestly.
03
🎯
Skill Assessment
A focused, 3–4 hour assessment for technical roles. Or a portfolio review and case discussion for creative/product roles. We pay ₹5,000 stipend for any assignment over 2 hours.
04
🤝
Offer & Join
Final conversation with the founding team. Offer sent within 24 hours of decision. We move fast — from first application to offer in under 3 weeks, usually 10 days.
