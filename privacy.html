Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 March 2026

SmartRent AI (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, profile picture.
  • Property Information: Listings you post (title, city, BHK, rent, images, description).
  • Usage Data: Chat messages, saved properties, preferences, search history.
  • Payment Data: UPI transaction ID, subscription plan details (we do not store sensitive bank details).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide platform services (property search, chat, AI recommendations).
  • To communicate with you regarding your account and listings.
  • To personalize AI suggestions based on your usage.
  • To process payments and subscriptions securely.
  • To improve platform features and experience.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Property owners’ contact information may be shared with users when you initiate chat or call. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights of SmartRent.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase with strict access rules. Sensitive data is encrypted, and access is limited to authorized personnel.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and correct your personal information.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting support.
  • Opt-out of promotional communications.

6. Children’s Privacy

SmartRent is not intended for users under 18 years old.

7. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries, contact: support@smartrent.ai
