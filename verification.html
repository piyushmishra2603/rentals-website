LEGAL & SECURITY

DATA PROTECTION

🔐
AES-256 Encryption
All uploaded documents are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage. Encryption keys are stored in separate HSMs (Hardware Security Modules). Zero-knowledge architecture means even our team cannot see your documents in plain text.
🛡️
DPDP Act 2023 Compliant
SmartRent AI is fully compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data at any time. Data is stored only on Indian servers (Mumbai, Hyderabad).
📋
Consent-Based Sharing
Your verification data (employment, financial, background) is NEVER shared without your explicit one-time consent per interaction. Owners cannot bulk-access your data — they can only request it on a property-enquiry basis.
🗑️
Right to Erasure
You can request deletion of all your personal data and uploaded documents from our platform at any time via Settings → Privacy → Delete My Data. Deletion is permanent and completes within 30 days per DPDP requirements.
🏛️
UIDAI & NSDL Integration
Our Aadhaar verification is conducted via licensed, UIDAI-approved Authentication User Agency (AUA) and eKYC User Agency (KUA) channels. We do not store Aadhaar numbers — only a cryptographic hash is retained.
🔍
ISO 27001 Certified
Our information security management system is ISO 27001:2022 certified, audited annually by independent third-party assessors. Penetration testing is conducted bi-annually. Bug bounty programme active for security researchers.

VERIFICATION FAQs

Is my Aadhaar number stored on your servers? ▼
No. We never store your 12-digit Aadhaar number. Only a one-way cryptographic hash is retained for matching purposes. We operate as an UIDAI-licensed eKYC User Agency and are legally prohibited from storing raw Aadhaar data. Our system processes your Aadhaar OTP in real time and immediately discards the raw data after verification.
How long does the full verification take? ▼
For tenants, the digital verification (Steps 1–4) takes 15–25 minutes in total. Background checks (Step 5) add 24–48 hours. For owners, digital steps take 20–30 minutes. The physical property inspection (Step 6) is scheduled within 48 hours and takes 45 minutes on-site. You'll receive a verified badge as soon as each step is completed — you don't have to wait for all steps.
Can I partially verify and use the platform? ▼
Yes. The platform operates on a tiered verification system. With just mobile OTP verification (Step 1), you can browse all listings. After Aadhaar KYC (Step 2), you can save listings and request callbacks. After complete verification, you get direct owner/tenant contact, AI matching, and the verified badge. Each tier unlocks incrementally — you don't have to complete everything before starting to use the platform.
What happens if my documents are rejected? ▼
If a document fails AI validation, you'll receive a specific reason (e.g., "document appears edited", "name mismatch with Aadhaar", "image quality too low"). You can re-upload corrected documents immediately. If you believe a rejection is incorrect, you can raise a dispute via Settings → Verification → Dispute a Decision. Our team manually reviews disputes within 48 hours.
Who can see my verification data and documents? ▼
Only you can see your full documents in your SmartRent AI dashboard. When you contact an owner, they see your Trust Score and verification badges — NOT your actual documents. If an owner requests to see your employment or financial proof, you'll receive a notification and must explicitly approve (or deny) that request. Our internal team can see a limited set of data for customer support purposes but cannot access encrypted documents.
How often do I need to re-verify? ▼
Identity verification (Aadhaar + PAN) is valid for 2 years. Property documents are valid for 1 year and must be updated if there are ownership changes. Employment and financial documents should be updated every 6 months for an accurate Trust Score. You'll receive automated reminders 30 days before expiry. Re-verification of unchanged documents is a quick 2-minute process — not a full repeat.
Is background check mandatory for tenants? ▼
No, the background check is optional but strongly recommended. Tenants with completed background checks receive enquiry acceptance rates 8× higher than unverified tenants and get first-priority access when multiple tenants enquire about the same property. Some premium property owners set "background-verified tenants only" — this requirement is always displayed clearly on the listing before you enquire.
